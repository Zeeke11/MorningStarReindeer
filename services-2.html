FAQs

Find answers to common questions about our magical reindeer experiences

How long are the reindeer visits?

Typical visits last between 60 to 90 minutes, depending on the package and event type. We ensure a comprehensive and engaging experience.

Are the reindeer safe around children?

Our reindeer are professionally trained and handled by experienced staff. We prioritize safety and gentle interactions for all ages.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve multiple regions. Contact us to confirm availability in your specific location.

Can we book for private events?

Absolutely. We specialize in private events including corporate parties, community gatherings, and personal celebrations.

What should we prepare?

We recommend having a designated area for the reindeer and ensuring proper ground conditions for their comfort.

How do we book a visit?

You can book online, by phone, or email. Our team will help you customize the perfect reindeer experience.
